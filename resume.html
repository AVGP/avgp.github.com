I am a highly-motivated jack of all trades, ready to go wherever needed to make the project fly.

I am willing to adapt and learn as well as exchange experience and share knowledge. I enjoy being challenged as much as I enjoy facilitating others to extend their horizont by adding fresh perspectives and pragmatic approaches to their skillset.

I am not a 9-to-5er, but a passionate team member taking ownership of my projects - I enjoy to help shaping the environment around me, instead of passively following.

How I work

My preferred way of working starts with identifying the actual goals and constraints. Once that is clear, I try to identify potential solution candidates.

This works best by building simple prototypes to show the different approaches.
I also try new ideas or technologies at this point.

Then it comes to planning and setting concrete milestones - getting ready for implementation. From there, we should be in a continuous development cycle, that goes "Try - Inspect - Adapt".

What I dislike
